FAQs

All your Questions Answered

Why do I need to hire Salespersons?

Sales is the lifeline of your Business. It is one of the ways to increase Revenue and grow your Business.


What am I paying for when I hire?

You're paying for a number of Amateur, Professional and/or Elite Salespersons. Note that the payment is our service fee for sourcing the Best and not their Commissions or Salaries.


How am I guaranteed they will perform as Expected?

Our Salespersons are vetted in-house and we have a strict selection process to ensure we filter through the crowd and source the Best.


Is the payment recurring?

The payment is a one-time payment per package selected. However, you can decide to order as many times as needed to scale.


Will I get a Refund if I have to fire some Hires or change my mind after Hiring?

We do not Refund. However, if the need arises to let go of salesperson(s), please let us know why.


Will the Salespersons be working Offline or just Online?

Our Salespersons can work both Online and/or Offline, depending on the needs of your business and your Agreement/Negotiation upon Hiring them.


What specific area(s) of Sales services do you offer?

Our Salespersons are proficient in Offline B2B, Offline B2C, Promotions, App Downloads and Conversion, Website Visits and Conversion, etc.


My Company is not based in Nigeria. Can I still Hire?

Our Salespersons are sourced from Nigeria but can Relocate if the need arises, based on the Terms of your Agreement upon Hiring.


Can I tell my friends, colleagues or partners about the Elite Sales Club?

We really appreciate Referrals. It enables us create value for more Businesses. Thank you.
